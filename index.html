Our Story

Yogod Marketing Agency was founded by Yosef, a 20-year-old student at Bahir Dar University with a big dream. What started as a passion for social media algorithms has grown into a dedicated agency serving real clients.

We are a brand new agency with zero completed projects yet, but we're currently working with 5 amazing clients who believed in our vision. We don't fake experience – we're building it from the ground up, one client at a time.

Our team of four brings together skills in graphic design, video editing, marketing strategy, and content creation. We may be young and new, but our ambition is unlimited and our skills are cutting-edge. Every client gets 200% of our energy because we're hungry to prove ourselves and build a reputation we can be proud of.

Based in Bahir Dar, Ethiopia, we're ready to help brands across the world grow their social media presence. Join us on this exciting journey!
